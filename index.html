ABOUT US

Thank you coming to our website! This website is managed by Kisoseiho high school students. We are conducting a survey about tourism in Kiso town in order to increase the number of foreign tourists. The number of visitors have been decreasing because of COVID-19, so we want to do something for our town. We hope many people understand our town more. Through this experience, it would be a great pleasure if you connect with us. Please share this page with your family and friends!
