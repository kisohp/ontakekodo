Experience Note

Mt.Ontake is one of the most sacred mountains and especially has been believed they have spiritual power. From long ago, people have worshiped Mt.Ontake. About 18 century, the trail of Mt.Onake opened and it enabled ordinary people to climb the mountain as a result, Ontake worship have been spread all around the countries. There are up to 20,000 stone monuments called reijin-hi which were built by believers, and that is a unique feature of Ontake worship.


The sacred trails toward Mt.Ontake is called Ontake-Kodo and we walked on there. We didn’t know Ontake-Kodo, so we worried about introducing it because it was hard to guess why so many tourists visit there every year. However, we could find many places fascinate us and some different ways to be more enjoyable trip and now we totally understand why it’s been loved by tourists for a long time.
